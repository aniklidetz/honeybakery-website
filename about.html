Our Story

The HoneyBakery Journey

HoneyBakery started from a love for baking and a deep appreciation for nature’s sweetest gifts—honey and herbs. Our journey began in a cozy kitchen, where we combined honey and hand-picked herbs to create unique and delicious treats. What started as a hobby has grown into a beloved community bakery, delighting taste buds and warming hearts with every honey-infused and herb-enhanced treat.

2010

Sarah and Michael Johnson founded HoneyBakery in their home kitchen, inspired by their love for natural ingredients and artisanal baking.

2012

We opened our first small storefront in Honey Grove, inviting the community to try our unique creations.

2015

We partnered with local beekeepers and herb growers to source our ingredients directly, ensuring the freshest and most flavorful products.

2021

We started our Honey and Herb Education Program, teaching the community about bees, sustainable beekeeping, and how to use herbs in cooking.

Today

We continue to innovate and create delicious honey and herb-infused treats for our wonderful community, staying true to our roots and commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our Mission

At HoneyBakery, our mission is to create delightful baked goods infused with honey and herbs that bring joy to our community while promoting sustainable beekeeping and herbal practices. We are dedicated to using the finest, locally-sourced ingredients and supporting our local ecosystem through responsible honey harvesting and herb cultivation. Each bite of our treats embodies our passion for natural, wholesome flavors and our love for the environment.
